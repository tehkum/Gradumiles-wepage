    B.I.T.C. Singapore

    About Us

    The Baking Industry Training College (BITC) is a well-respected institution in Singapore, recognized for its quality education by various reputable organizations. It has been accredited by SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG) as an Approved Training Organization (ATO) and is registered with the Committee for Private Education (CPE) as a Private Education Institution (PEI). Furthermore, BITC has received the 4-Year EduTrust Award, which is a voluntary certification that acknowledges the excellence of private education providers in Singapore. The college boasts a highly qualified teaching staff and state-of-the-art resources and facilities, making its diplomas and certificates highly sought after by graduates looking to gain an edge in their professional careers.
