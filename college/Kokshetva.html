About Us

Kokshetau State University is a modern educational institution founded in 1962 in the city of Kokshetau, Kazakhstan. The university offers multi-level programs in various specialties including bachelor's degree (65 specialties), master's degree (30 specialties), doctorate (6 specialties) in the field of natural sciences and general medicine. The university was named in honor of Mr. Ulikhanov, one of the great Kazakh scientists, historians, ethnographers, geographers, translators and travelers. MBBS research in Kazakhstan, especially Kokshetau State University, provides great opportunities for medical students and practical knowledge at an affordable price which is a great reference for international students.

One of the most popular and oldest medical universities in Kazakhstan Established in 1962, KG Medical University is in a class by itself and Premium Medical University in Kazakhstan. It offers the best curriculum developed according to International Education Structure. Its medical courses are approved by highly Reputed Organizations like World Health Organization, Medical Council of India, United Nation Organization, UNESCO, etc, KGU is the Superior medical university in Kazakhstan. Eligiblity Criteria

To be considered for admission, students must satisfy the following requirements:

  • A minimum of 50% in chemistry and biology in the 12th grade if in general category. For others the minimum requirement is 40%.
  • Qualification of National Eligibility Entrance Test (NEET)
