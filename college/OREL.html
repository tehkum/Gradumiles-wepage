    Orel State Medical University

    About Us

    Orel State Medical University (OSMU) is a reputable medical institution located in the city of Orel, Russia. Founded in 1885, the university has a long history of providing high-quality medical education to students from around the world. OSMU offers undergraduate and graduate programs in medicine, dentistry, and pharmacy, as well as postgraduate training for medical professionals. The university is known for its emphasis on practical learning and hands-on experience, and its faculty is made up of experienced medical professionals. OSMU's modern facilities and cutting-edge research contribute to prepare students to become leaders in the field of medicine and healthcare. The university is well-equipped with modern infrastructure and resources to support the students' learning and research.

    Eligiblity Criteria

    To be considered for admission, students must satisfy the following requirements:

    • The applicant must be of 18 years of age or above on the date of admission
    • The applicant must have secured an aggregate of at least 50% in 10+2 or equivalent examination in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology

    Campus Life

    Orel State Medical University (OSMU) offers an exciting campus life with its numerous clubs, associations, and activities. The university has a student government that strives to make student life enjoyable and meaningful. This includes organizing events like sports competitions, talent shows, conferences and seminars, and festivals. OSMU also has a student newspaper, art clubs, and student-run radio and television stations.

    Hostel and Facilities

    Orel State Medical University offers a range of hostel and facilities for its students, including accommodation, healthcare, sports, and leisure.

    Accommodation: The university offers a range of hostel accommodation for its students with single, double, and triple occupancy rooms. The hostels are equipped with all the necessary amenities, including TV, Wi-Fi, a shared kitchen, and a laundry room. The university also offers a variety of student housing options, such as shared apartments, student flats, and private accommodation.

    Healthcare: Orel State Medical University provides quality on-campus healthcare facilities that include a 24-hour emergency service, a general hospital, a dental clinic, a laboratory, and a pharmacy. The university also offers a variety of health insurance plans to its students.

    Sports and Leisure: The university has a wide range of sports and leisure facilities, including a swimming pool, tennis court, basketball court, football field, and volleyball court. The university also organizes a variety of cultural and entertainment activities, including concerts, theatrical performances, and movie nights.

    The university also provides a range of clubs and societies for its students, such as the Student Union, which organizes various cultural and social activities throughout the academic year.
