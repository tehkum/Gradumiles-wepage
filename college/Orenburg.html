    Orenburg State Medical University

    About Us

    Orenburg State Medical University (OSMU) is a leading medical institution located in the city of Orenburg, Russia. Founded in 1944, the university has a rich history of providing high-quality medical education to students from all over the world. OSMU offers undergraduate and graduate programs in medicine, dentistry, and pharmacy, as well as postgraduate training for medical professionals. The university is known for its strong emphasis on practical learning and hands-on experience, and its faculty is made up of highly qualified and experienced medical professionals. OSMU is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and cutting-edge technology to support the students' learning and research. With its focus on preparing students to become leaders in the field of medicine and healthcare, OSMU is a reputable and well-respected institution in the medical education field.

    Eligiblity Criteria

    To be considered for admission, students must satisfy the following requirements:

    • The student should have passed the class 12th examination from a recognized board securing a minimum of 50% marks in Physics/Chemistry/ Biology and 40% for the reserved category.
    • They should be in good physical and mental health which enables the student to study.
    • Students can pay tuition fees, cost of accommodation in Hostel, Life and health insurance,as well as the living expenses during the whole period of academics in Russia.
    • Students should qualify NEET for general category 50 %, for SC /ST/OBC it is 40%.
    • The Students shall complete the age of 17 years on 31st December of the year of admission.

    Documents Required

    These given documents should be ensured for the admission and visa process:

    • Notarized Certificate of Secondary Education.
    • Notarized Certificate copy attested by Ministry of Human Resource Development and legalized by Russian Embassy In foreign country.
    • Notary attested copy of birth certificate.
    • Medical certificate with all necessary laboratory test results including AIDS test.
    • Copy of National passport.
    • 2 recent passport-sized photographs in matt paper only.

    Campus Life

    Orenburg State Medical University (OSMU) offers a diverse and vibrant campus life for its students. The university is located in the city of Orenburg, which is the administrative center of the Orenburg region. The university prides itself on its diverse and stimulating student life. With a wide range of sports, arts, and cultural activities, there is something for everyone.

    Hostel and Facilities

    Orenburg State Medical University offers its students a range of facilities and services to make their stay more comfortable. The university has a spacious hostel which provides comfortable accommodation for students who prefer to stay on campus. The hostel is equipped with modern amenities and is located close to the university. It features single, double and triple rooms with attached bathrooms and a range of other facilities.

    The university also provides a range of other facilities to its students. These include a library, a canteen, a computer lab, a sports hall, a gymnasium, a lecture hall, and a conference hall. The library is well stocked with books and journals and also provides access to online databases. The canteen offers a variety of dishes and beverages at affordable prices. The computer lab is equipped with the latest hardware and software and provides access to the internet. The sports hall has a wide range of sports equipment and facilities for different sports activities. The gymnasium has the latest equipment to help students stay fit. The lecture hall and conference hall are equipped with modern audio-visual equipment and provide a comfortable environment for lectures and seminars.

    The university also provides a number of other services such as health care, student counseling and career guidance.
