    Student Life

    The standard of living in France is very high and one thing is certain. Studying in France will be a wonderful experience that will change your life.

    France has something for every taste, and it's not just for wine and cheese lovers. Whether you prefer a busy urban setting, the quiet mountains or the laid-back atmosphere of the Mediterranean coast, there is a destination for you. Paris offers a unique metropolitan atmosphere in an enchanting setting. Marseille and Montpellier are just steps away from the Mediterranean coast. Includes summer beach. Rennes and Nantes are close to the rugged Atlantic coast. From Grenoble and Lyon, the Alps are within reach.
